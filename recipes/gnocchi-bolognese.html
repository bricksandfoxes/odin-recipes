Ingredients Needed:

  • Bacon This soup starts with bacon cooked in a skillet
  • Butter One of the core ingredients in most dishes
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Basked Potatoes
  • Green Onions
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream

    • Instructions:

    1. Cook the bacon
    2. Melt the butter, then whisk in flour and milk
    3. Add the potatoes and green onion, bring to a boil
    4. Reduce to a simmer, then stir in the remaining ingredients
    5. Cook until the cheese melts
